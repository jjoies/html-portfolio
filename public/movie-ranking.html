The Best Movies According to Jihae

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

This is one of my favorite Studio Ghibli movies! I've watched this a ton as a kid. I love the story, the characters, and the art style.

The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes

I recently watched this movie because I grew up reading and watching the original Hunger Games Trilogy. The story was so captivating and I loved learning about the early days in the HG world.

Tangled

I love Disney's princess movies, but I think this is my favorite one. The songs are great and the characters are so loveable!
